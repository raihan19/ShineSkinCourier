Years of Experience

Runner Courier Service Ltd is the best E-commerce logistics solution in Bangladesh with the experience, capabilities, functional expertise, technological embedment for integrated logistics solutions or One Umbrella Solutions to the e-commerce industry.

Runner Courier Service Ltd aims to aid the expanding e-commerce sector of Bangladesh providing tech-first logistics support with exclusive features and talented workforce, Runner Courier Service Ltd set to give the delivery service industry of Bangladesh a brand new pace. Our service to ensure maximum comfort and peace of mind for the online merchants, we have built a basket of tailored features and services. The products in this basket range from picking up products from merchants’ address to collecting COD amount from customers’ doorstep and depositing back to merchants’ account - so that the merchant can completely focus on growing and strengthening his core business without worrying about the logistics. We are integrating the merchants in a smart software system, where you can manage all activities of the delivery. The first courier company in Bangladesh where everything is managed by automated software. Our goal, mission & vision is to provide best quality service for satisfying customers.
